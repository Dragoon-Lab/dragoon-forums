Custom Models





Note: If the selected model is from your assigned group, it will open in author mode. Otherwise, it will open in student mode.

Dragoon only supports one author at a time, and saves automatically as you work.

The History button can be used to reload old sessions. This can be useful if another author in your group saved over your work accidentally.

You can use the "Explanation" button inside the node editor to add documentation for an individual node.
